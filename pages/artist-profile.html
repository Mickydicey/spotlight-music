In a small studio in Enugu, 22-year-old Chinemerem is quietly making soulful Afrobeats.

About

Born and raised in Enugu, Chinemerem blends traditional Igbo melodies with modern Afrobeats production. His debut EP "Adanna" is a love letter to Eastern Nigeria and the sound of a new generation finding its voice.
